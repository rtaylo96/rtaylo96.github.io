Project Design


Project Overview

Describe your application -

This website application is for my client Bhavisha Naik, who is the district coordinator for Rotaract Clubs at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte (UNCC) and Central Piedmont Community College (CPCC).The purpose of this webpage, is to talk about what the district of these two schools mean and explaining what Rotaract Club does for the community.

Intended Users of the Website -

The intended users of this website are going to be District 7680 Rotaract Members, Students who are a part of the Rotaract Club at UNCC and CPCC, and potential new members.

Overview of the Content of the Website -

Overall, this website will be supporting the community service clubs in the Rotaract District 7680 who Bhavisha is the coordinator of and being able to help spread awareness about the clubs on the two college campuses that Bhavisha communicates with. This webpage will also be a way to attract new members who are interested in signing up and how they can get in contact with the club locally on their campus or contact Bhavisha directly for any inquiries about joining Rotaract.
